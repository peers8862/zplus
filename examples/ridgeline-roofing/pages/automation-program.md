# Automation & AI

Ridgeline is deliberately becoming an **Automated Enterprise**: we hand routine back-office
work to automations and AI agents, and we keep the division of duties documented so the
owners always understand what's running.

- **The maturity ladder:** every automation and agent is rated `manual → assisted →
  supervised → autonomous`. See the boards on the **Automations** and **Agents** landings.
- **Division of duties:** who does what across human / AI / machine — see
  [Division of duties](division-of-duties.md).
- **Guardrails:** agents are bound by **Policies** (e.g. Financial Controls) and every
  automation names the systems it touches, so nothing runs unaccountably.

Today: 6 automations and 4 agents in production. The **Action Center** surfaces anything
open or unowned; **Incidents** records what broke and what we learned.
