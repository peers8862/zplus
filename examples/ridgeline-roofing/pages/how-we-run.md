# How We Run

The functional handbook — how each area of Ridgeline operates day to day. The recurring
**Procedures** collection holds the step-by-step SOPs (grouped by function on its landing);
this page is the map.

- **Finance & accounting** — QuickBooks bookkeeping, Stripe deposits, Gusto payroll,
  monthly close. See the Monthly Close and Vendor Onboarding procedures.
- **Sales & estimating** — EagleView measurements → JobNimbus estimate → signed contract.
  See Job Estimate to Contract.
- **Production** — crew scheduling, material staging, CompanyCam documentation, punch-out.
- **Warranty & claims** — storm damage insurance claims and warranty handling.
- **People** — hiring and onboarding through Gusto and Google Workspace.

Systems we run on are in the **Systems** registry; who owns what is in **Roles**.
