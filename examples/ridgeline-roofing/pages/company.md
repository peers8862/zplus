# Company

**Ridgeline Roofing Co.** is a residential and commercial roofing contractor serving the
greater metro area since 2016. We install and repair asphalt shingle, metal, and flat
commercial roofs, and we run a storm-response practice for insurance claims.

- **~25 people** — office, estimating, and field crews.
- **~$3M** annual revenue, growing ~20%/yr.
- Two crews plus subcontracted overflow during storm season.
