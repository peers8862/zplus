# Mission, vision & values

**Mission.** Keep every customer dry with roofs that outlast the warranty — installed
safely, billed honestly, and backed after the sale.

**Vision.** Become the metro's most trusted roofer by making our work transparent: to
customers, to crews, and to the systems and agents that increasingly run the back office.

**Values.**

- **Safety first** — nobody gets hurt for a deadline (see the Safety & OSHA policy).
- **Honest paper** — estimates, invoices, and warranties say exactly what we'll do.
- **Leave it better** — cleaner site, tighter flashing, happier neighbor.
- **Document as we automate** — if a machine does it, it's written down and traceable.
